How predictions work

Each route is scored 0–100 using live data from Open-Meteo. Where real CalMac cancellation history is available, the weather score is blended with historical reliability (70% weather, 30% history) and capped at the route's real-world track record. Sailing decisions are made by CalMac — these are estimates only.
